Section 01

Problem & User

Account managers at Ajaia manage 20–80 client and prospect relationships simultaneously. Today, critical relationship context — commitments made, conversation tone, last contact dates — lives across email threads, personal notes, and memory. There is no single source of truth.

"We discover a relationship has gone cold only after it matters. By then, re-engagement is awkward and the opportunity is often lost."

Core Pain Points

Fragmented contextManually scanning email threads before calls15–30 min per account review

Reactive re-engagementCold relationships discovered too lateDamaged trust; lost opportunities

Section 03

End-to-End Workflow

📨 Data Sources
  • Gmail threads (OAuth)
  • Google Calendar events
  • Manual notes (in-app)
  • Account metadata
↓
🤖 AI Processing Layer — Automated
  • Extract thread summaries & key commitments
  • Detect tone signals (warm / neutral / cooling)
  • Infer account stage from conversation content
  • Calculate days-since-contact & engagement velocity
  • Draft personalized follow-up message
↓
🗄️ Account Intelligence Store
  • Structured record per account (updated on each ingestion cycle)
  • Fields: summary · stage · last contact · sentiment · next action · thread refs
  • Immutable history log for full audit trail
↓
📊 Dashboard
  • Account list by health/priority
  • Detail card per account
  • Alert feed (inline)
🔔 Alert & Digest Engine
  • Re-engagement queue
  • Weekly pipeline digest
  • Slack/email notifications
↓
👤 Human Review & Action — Always Required
  • Review AI-drafted messages before sending
  • Confirm or reject suggested stage changes
  • Dismiss or snooze alerts
  • Add manual context notes
  • AI never sends. Humans always send.

Automation vs. Human Decision Matrix

TaskAutomatedHuman-GatedRationale
Ingest email threadsAutoPurely mechanical data retrieval
Summarize conversationAutoAI reads, never writes to record without review
Detect re-engagement signalsAutoRule + AI; human confirms via action
Draft follow-up messageAutoHuman editsAuthenticity — tone must be owner's voice
Stage change suggestionSuggestHuman confirmsStage has downstream implications
Send messageHuman onlyRelationship risk; non-negotiable human gate
Log notesHuman onlyContext nuance only owner knows
